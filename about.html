History

Bananas have been grown for thousands of years, originally from Southeast Asia.

Nutritional Benefits

  • Rich in potassium
  • Good source of fiber
  • Boosts energy

Farming Steps

  1. Prepare soil
  2. Plant banana suckers
  3. Water regularly
  4. Harvest when ripe

Banana Varieties

Cavendish
Most common type found in stores worldwide.
Plantain
Starchy bananas used for cooking.
Red Banana
Sweeter with a reddish skin.
